Your happy place awaits...


Situated in Orikum, Albania, Vila Devis offers peaceful city and mountain views in a comfortable and welcoming setting. This charming guesthouse features a spacious terrace and air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and private bathrooms, providing a relaxed 4-star experience. Each room is equipped with a kettle, flat-screen TV, and safety deposit box. Some rooms also include a balcony, and select ones offer beautiful sea views. Staff at the reception speak both English and Italian, ensuring a friendly and helpful stay for all guests.

Our Facilities


Free Parking

The hotel has a convenient and complimentary parking facility, offering guests a stress-free experience with ample space, 24-hour security, and easy access to the main entrance.

Free Wifi

The hotel offers complimentary Wi-Fi throughout its premises, ensuring guests stay connected with high-speed internet access in their rooms, common areas, and even by the pool.

Family Rooms

The hotel features spacious family rooms, thoughtfully designed with multiple beds, cozy seating areas, and kid-friendly amenities, providing a comfortable and welcoming environment.

Facilities for disabled guests

The hotel provides exceptional facilities for disabled guests, including wheelchair-accessible rooms, elevators, and bathrooms equipped with grab bars, ensuring a comfortable and accommodating stay for everyone.
